About ScholarViews

ScholarViews was established by Abhee Jani and Asmita Pramanik during their junior years at UIUC. Driven by their personal experiences and the observed need for interview preparation assistance, they created a platform to aid individuals in honing their interview skills.

Both founders recognized the challenges faced by job seekers in showcasing their abilities effectively during interviews. Their goal was to bridge this gap by offering tailored mock interviews and expert guidance, providing the necessary tools for success.

Abhee and Asmita's dedication to helping others navigate the interview process stemmed from their own journey. They envisioned a space where individuals could refine their skills, gain confidence, and ultimately excel in securing their desired positions.

At ScholarViews, the founders remain committed to empowering individuals to convert preparation into success, driven by their belief that honed skills pave the way for professional triumph.
